    Why Release Pro?

    Release Pro helps Engineering teams keep track of their releases throughout the release process. The more information a team member can see about the release, the better a release can be for your users.

    One Stop Shop For Your Release Process

    You no longer have to hit up all the different slack channels or other team members to get up to date information on current releases or any previous release. You'll be able to clearly view what went into a release. Knowing what went into a release and when it was released will help the productivity of any engineering team.

    Keep Track of iOS and Android

    Releases processes for iOS and Android are completely different. The way you track them shouldn't be. Whether you submit a release candidate to the App Store or Google Play Store you can rest assure that you'll know everything there is to your release.

    Keep a Historical Record of Your Releases

    What release are we on? Whats the last beta build that was cut? What build had a certain feature? Take the guess work out of it by being able to see clearly all previous releases from your team.

    Know Which Team Owns Which Feature

    Who owns this feature? Can someone link me to the tech spec? Which PM should I reach out to for some clarification around this new feature? The Features Owner Doc has all your answers. The Features Owner Doc is your source of truth for all teams and projects at your company. You no longer need to waste time by trying to figure out who owns which project.
